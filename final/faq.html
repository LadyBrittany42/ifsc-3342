Frequently Asked Questions

What are Social Saturdays?
Social Saturdays are lunch get-togethers at local restaurants. We use these meetings to discuss topics or mini-lessons to both share knowledge and evoke conversations over a meal.
What can I expect from your rituals?
Ever ritual is different, depending on the leader and their path, but are open to all for observation or participation. We don't require formal dress although we welcome it. Points of ritual etiquette are usually outlined before the ritual begins so being on time is important too!
Is your group family friendly?
We are! Our group welcomes the knowledge of all of our community members, and that includes the young'ins!
